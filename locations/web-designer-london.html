Local SEO for London Businesses

Getting found by London customers on Google requires a specific local SEO strategy. Every website we build for a London business includes:

  • Google Business Profile optimisation: Correct category, complete information, location-relevant keywords, and photo uploads to improve Maps visibility.
  • London-specific meta tags: Page titles and descriptions that include your borough or area — e.g., "Plumber in Hackney" rather than just "Plumber".
  • Schema markup for local businesses: Structured data that tells Google exactly where you operate and what you do.
  • Location-relevant content: Pages and copy that reference your specific London area, building relevance for local search queries.
  • Citation consistency: Consistent NAP (name, address, phone) across your website, Google Business, and key directories.
