What Every MazTechDesigns Website Includes

We do not cut corners. Every website we build — regardless of budget — includes the following as standard:

  • Mobile-first design: Over 60% of UK web traffic is on mobile. Your site will look and perform perfectly on every screen size.
  • Fast load times: We optimise images, minimise code, and use clean HTML/CSS to achieve sub-2-second load times. Google rewards fast sites with higher rankings.
  • On-page SEO foundations: Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3), meta titles and descriptions, canonical URLs, alt text on images, and a structured data setup where appropriate.
  • SSL and HTTPS: Every site is secured with an SSL certificate — this is a ranking signal and a trust signal for visitors.
  • Contact form with email delivery: A working contact form that actually sends emails to your inbox, not a form that looks nice but never delivers.
  • Google Analytics and Search Console setup: So you can see exactly how many people visit your site, where they come from, and which pages they land on.
  • Clean, maintainable code: We write clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no bloated page builders, no plug-in dependency nightmares.
  • Browser compatibility: Tested across Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Our Web Design Process

We follow a structured process that keeps projects on track and ensures the final result exceeds your expectations:

  1. Discovery Call (Free): We learn about your business, goals, target audience, and existing digital presence. This call is free and carries no obligation.
  2. Proposal & Scope: Within 24–48 hours, you receive a clear proposal outlining deliverables, timeline, and pricing. No surprises.
  3. Design Concepts: We create initial design mockups for your homepage and key sections. You review and provide feedback.
  4. Build & Development: Once design is approved, we build the full site — responsive, fast, and SEO-ready.
  5. Content Integration: Your text, images, and branding are integrated. We can also write copy for an additional fee if needed.
  6. Review & Revisions: You get two full rounds of revisions included. We refine until you are fully satisfied.
  7. Testing & Launch: We test across devices and browsers, set up analytics, and launch your site on your domain.
  8. Handover & Support: You receive a full handover with everything you need to manage and grow your site going forward.

Web Design FAQs

How much does a small business website cost in the UK?

At MazTechDesigns, small business websites start from £499 for a clean, professional 5-page site. More complex sites with custom functionality range from £800–£2,500. We provide a transparent, itemised quote before any work begins — no surprises.

How long does it take to build a small business website?

Most small business websites are completed within 2–4 weeks. Timelines depend on how quickly content (text and images) is provided and how many revision rounds are needed. We provide a clear project timeline at the start of every engagement.

Will my website rank on Google?

Every website we build includes solid on-page SEO foundations: proper heading structure, keyword-rich meta tags, fast load times, mobile optimisation, and clean code. This gives your site the best possible foundation for ranking. We also offer SEO advice through our blog and ongoing SEO support as a separate service.

Do I need to provide my own content?

Ideally yes — you know your business best. However, we can write professional website copy for an additional fee of £150–£350 depending on the number of pages. We can also source stock photography if you do not have your own images.

Do you build websites outside of London?

Yes — we work with businesses across the entire UK, including Manchester, Birmingham, Leeds, Bristol, Glasgow, Cardiff, and everywhere in between. All work is done remotely, so location is never a barrier.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely. Website redesigns are one of our most common projects. If your current site looks outdated, loads slowly, or is not bringing in enquiries, we can rebuild it from scratch while preserving your existing domain authority and search rankings.

What platform will my website be built on?

This depends on your needs. We build on HTML/CSS/JS for lightweight, fast-loading sites, WordPress for content-heavy sites where you need to manage your own blog, and Shopify for e-commerce. We recommend the platform best suited to your goals, not the one that is easiest for us.
